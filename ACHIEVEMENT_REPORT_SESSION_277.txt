WHAT NOODLE 2 PROVIDES
================================================================================

Noodle 2 is a comprehensive safety-aware orchestration system for large-scale
physical design experimentation with OpenROAD.

## Core Capabilities

**Safety & Control:**
- Three safety domains: sandbox, guarded, locked
- ECO class restrictions per domain
- Pre-execution legality validation
- Run legality reports with violation details
- Study blocking on illegal configurations

**Multi-Stage Workflows:**
- Arbitrary N-stage experiment graphs
- Sequential stage execution with safety gates
- Survivor selection and advancement
- Trial budget enforcement per stage
- Stage-specific ECO class allowances
- Deterministic case naming: <case>_<stage>_<derived>

**Execution & Orchestration:**
- Ray-based distributed execution
- Docker-integrated OpenROAD tooling
- Container: efabless/openlane:ci2504-dev-amd64
- Parallel trial execution within stages
- Fault-tolerant task scheduling
- Resource management and isolation

**Observability & Telemetry:**
- Comprehensive artifact management
- JSON-LD metadata and indexing
- Study/stage/case telemetry hierarchies
- Early failure detection and classification
- Structured logging and reports
- Artifact bundle generation per trial

**Analysis & Metrics:**
- Timing analysis (WNS, TNS extraction)
- Congestion metrics (hot bins, ratios)
- ECO effectiveness tracking
- Prior accumulation (trusted/mixed/suspicious)
- Case lineage and provenance tracking
- Safety trace records

## Supported PDKs

- **Nangate45** (verified, fast bring-up target)
- **ASAP7** (verified, with documented workarounds)
- **Sky130/sky130A** (verified, OpenLane compatible)

## Advanced Features

- Configurable survivor selection strategies
- Study abortion and downstream blocking
- Cross-target validation and consistency
- Artifact indexing with deep links
- Policy-driven control and budgets
- Deterministic and reproducible execution
- Extensible architecture for future enhancements

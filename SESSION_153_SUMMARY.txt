## WHAT IS NOODLE 2?

Noodle 2 is a **safety-aware, policy-driven orchestration system** for
large-scale physical-design experimentation built on top of OpenROAD.

### Purpose
Noodle 2 manages *uncertainty, failure, and limited compute budgets* while
exploring Engineering Change Orders (ECOs) across complex, multi-stage design
workflows.

### Key Differentiator
Noodle 2 does NOT replace PD tools or algorithms. Instead, it provides a
**deterministic control plane** that enables:
- Structured experimentation
- Auditable decision-making
- Safe automation across multiple studies, stages, and design variants

### Target Users
- Physical design researchers
- VLSI engineers exploring design space
- Academic institutions conducting chip design research
- Industrial teams optimizing existing designs


## WHAT NOODLE 2 IS

Noodle 2 is a **safety-aware, policy-driven orchestration system** for
large-scale physical-design experimentation built on OpenROAD.

**It is NOT:**
- A physical design tool
- An optimizer or solver
- An ECO generator
- An OpenROAD replacement

**It IS:**
- A control plane for PD experimentation
- A safety-critical experiment controller
- A policy-driven decision engine
- An auditable orchestration layer

**Core Value Proposition:**
- Decides what to try, when to stop, and what to trust
- Manages uncertainty, failure, and compute budgets
- Enables structured experimentation with safety guarantees
- Provides deterministic, reproducible, auditable workflows

**Production Features:**
- Multi-stage refinement (arbitrary N-stage graphs)
- Safety domains (sandbox/guarded/locked)
- Distributed execution (Ray-based)
- Comprehensive telemetry and reporting
- Docker-containerized tool execution
- ECO effectiveness tracking with memory
- Case lineage and DAG management
- Early failure classification
- Abort thresholds and rails

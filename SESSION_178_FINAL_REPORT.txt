═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  WHAT IS NOODLE 2?
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Noodle 2 is a comprehensive safety-aware orchestration system for large-scale
physical design experimentation with OpenROAD. It provides:

CORE CAPABILITIES
─────────────────
• Safety domains (sandbox, guarded, locked) with ECO class constraints
• Multi-stage refinement with survivor selection
• Deterministic case lineage tracking (DAG-based)
• Policy-driven early stopping mechanisms
• Ray-based distributed execution
• Comprehensive telemetry and artifact management
• Docker-based OpenROAD integration
• PDK support (Nangate45, ASAP7, Sky130)

ARCHITECTURAL HIGHLIGHTS
────────────────────────
• Case management with deterministic naming (<case>_<stage>_<derived>)
• Safety policy enforcement preventing dangerous ECOs
• Prior belief tracking for ECO effectiveness
• Timing-driven ECO targeting from critical path analysis
• Git-based provenance for full reproducibility
• JSON-LD metadata for research publication
• Plateau detection for early termination
• Anomaly detection in timing/congestion metrics

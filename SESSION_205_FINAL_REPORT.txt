What is Noodle 2?

  Noodle 2 is a safety-aware, policy-driven orchestration system for
  large-scale physical design experimentation built on top of OpenROAD.

  It provides a deterministic control plane that enables structured
  experimentation, auditable decision-making, and safe automation across
  multiple studies, stages, and design variants.

Key Differentiators:

  • Does NOT replace PD tools - it orchestrates them
  • Manages uncertainty and failure in complex workflows
  • Enforces safety constraints on design modifications
  • Enables reproducible research with comprehensive telemetry
  • Supports multi-stage refinement with survivor selection
  • Provides deterministic case lineage tracking

Primary Use Cases:

  1. Large-scale ECO exploration across complex PD workflows
  2. Multi-stage design refinement with safety constraints
  3. Reproducible research with comprehensive telemetry
  4. Safe automation of physical design experimentation
  5. Policy-driven optimization with early stopping

Target Users:

  • Physical design researchers
  • ASIC/SoC design teams
  • OpenROAD users
  • Academic research groups
  • Design automation engineers

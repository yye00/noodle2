                       WHAT IS NOODLE 2?
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Noodle 2 is a safety-aware, policy-driven orchestration system for large-scale
physical design (PD) experimentation built on top of OpenROAD.

Key Purpose:
  Manage uncertainty, failure, and limited compute budgets while exploring
  Engineering Change Orders (ECOs) across complex, multi-stage design workflows.

Positioning:
  Noodle 2 operates ABOVE physical design tools like OpenROAD. It doesn't
  replace PD algorithms—it decides what to try, when to stop, and what to trust.

Core Value:
  Acts as a safety-critical experiment controller rather than an optimizer,
  providing deterministic control, auditable decisions, and safe automation.

================================================================================
                          WHAT IS NOODLE 2?
================================================================================

Noodle 2 is a safety-aware, policy-driven orchestration system for large-scale
physical design experimentation built on top of OpenROAD.

Key Value Proposition:
  Noodle 2 does NOT replace PD tools or algorithms. Instead, it provides a
  deterministic control plane that enables structured experimentation,
  auditable decision-making, and safe automation across multiple studies,
  stages, and design variants.

Primary Use Cases:
  • Managing uncertainty and failure in PD experimentation
  • Exploring Engineering Change Orders (ECOs) across complex workflows
  • Safe automation with policy-driven constraints
  • Multi-stage design refinement with survivor selection
  • Reproducible research with comprehensive telemetry

Target Users:
  • Physical design researchers
  • ASIC/SoC design teams
  • OpenROAD users
  • Academic research groups
  • Design automation engineers

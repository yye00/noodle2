PRODUCT DESCRIPTION
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Noodle 2 is a safety-aware, policy-driven orchestration system for large-scale
physical design experimentation built on top of OpenROAD. It manages uncertainty,
failure, and limited compute budgets while exploring Engineering Change Orders
(ECOs) across complex, multi-stage design workflows.

KEY CAPABILITIES
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
✅ Safety Domains
   • sandbox - exploratory, permissive
   • guarded - default, production-like
   • locked - conservative, regression-only

✅ Multi-Stage Execution
   • Arbitrary N-stage experiment graphs
   • Per-stage policy binding
   • Per-stage safety evaluation
   • Survivor selection and advancement

✅ Case Management
   • Deterministic case naming (case_stage_index)
   • DAG-based lineage tracking
   • Immutable snapshot management
   • Artifact bundling and indexing

✅ ECO Classification

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                           WHAT IS NOODLE 2?
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Noodle 2 is a safety-aware, policy-driven orchestration system for large-scale
physical design experimentation built on OpenROAD.

KEY CAPABILITIES:
├─ Safety Domains: sandbox/guarded/locked with ECO constraints
├─ Multi-Stage Refinement: Staged optimization with survivor selection
├─ Case Lineage: Deterministic DAG-based tracking
├─ Policy Control: Early stopping and resource management
├─ Distributed Execution: Ray-based parallel processing
├─ Telemetry: Comprehensive metrics and artifact management
├─ OpenROAD Integration: Docker-based execution
└─ Multi-PDK: Nangate45, ASAP7, Sky130 support
